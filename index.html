You can pin your most frequently used or favorite menus on the top left panel of your ITS dashboard. This way you will be able to access them at a glance by simply clicking on them. To do this, first type in the name of the menu (e.g. USERS-9) in the menu search box on the left panel, then click on the plus (+) sign above next to the “X” sign to pin the menu. Repeat this process until you have all your favorites pinned at the top. Ps: The procedure for deleting a menu from the pinned list is the same. However, to remove a menu, you must use the “X” sign instead.
